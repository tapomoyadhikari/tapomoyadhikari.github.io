News

December 2023: I am pleased to announce the successful publication of the very first research paper from Corgnit Research India's Lab. The paper, titled Design and Development of a Secure and Efficient Authentication System for Mobile Devices , introduces an innovative authentication system tailored specifically for mobile devices. This system incorporates advanced security measures, including multi-factor authentication and biometric techniques, to overcome the security challenges associated with conventional password, PIN, and token-based authentication methods. The paper has been published in the well-esteemed Journal of Global Research in Computer Science, appearing in Volume 14, Issue 4, dated December 2023.
