About

Tapomoy Adhikari, a distinguished researcher of Computer Science and Engineering, holding the esteemed position of Principal Researcher at Corgnit Research India, a prominent and forward-thinking institution based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. With an unwavering commitment to technological advancement and an insatiable curiosity, Adhikari's remarkable journey in this domain has been marked by significant contributions, particularly in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).

Adhikari's remarkable expertise in AI and ML is exemplified by his tireless dedication to developing intelligent systems and intricate algorithms that simulate human-like cognitive abilities. His work continually pushes the boundaries of innovation, pioneering novel advancements in these fields. Specifically, his contributions encompass the adept utilization of statistical models and advanced algorithms within Machine Learning, enabling precise predictions and invaluable insights derived from complex datasets.

Corgnit Research India, under Adhikari's visionary leadership, stands as a prestigious CS Research lab dedicated to fostering groundbreaking innovations. His role in establishing the Virtual AI and Generative AI research lab has been instrumental in shaping Corgnit's R&D landscape, paving the way for pioneering advancements in AI. His contributions reflect an unyielding commitment to driving innovation and progress in AI, ML, and emerging technologies, with a steadfast goal of fostering meaningful advancements in the realm of technology.

Most Cited Papers

C - 01
Adhikari, T. (2023). MeloHarmony: Exploring Emotion in Crafting AI-Generated Music with Generative Adversarial Network Powered Harmony [Corgnit Research India]
Retrieved from https://doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4575257
          This research explores the transformative intersection of music and artificial intelligence (AI), particularly
        in the realm of emotional resonance. It emphasizes how AI, especially Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs),
        can enhance the emotional depth of AI-generated music compositions. The paper traces the historical significance
        of emotions in music and the contemporary fusion of AI and human sentiment. It presents a novel framework that
        leverages GANs to infuse emotions into AI-generated harmonies, showcasing technology's pivotal role in elevating
        the emotive quality of music.


        The research covers various aspects, including data collection, GAN architecture, emotional embedding
        techniques, and the training process. It also analyzes the emotional impact of AI-generated music on human
        perception, both quantitatively and qualitatively. Ethical considerations and practical applications of the
        GAN-powered methodology are addressed, promising a symphony of insights that harmonize AI innovation with human
        emotional depth in music composition.

C - 02
Adhikari, T. (2023). Design and Development of a Secure and Efficient Authentication System for Mobile Devices [Corgnit Research India].
Retrieved from https://doi.org/10.36227/techrxiv.23820642.v1
          The proliferation of mobile technology has transformed the way I communicate, access information, 
          and perform various activities. However, the increasing usage of mobile devices also introduces 
          new challenges in terms of security and user authentication. Traditional password-based authentication 
          mechanisms are often vulnerable to attacks such as brute-force, phishing, and dictionary attacks. 
          Moreover, the limited input capabilities and small form factor of mobile devices impose additional 
          constraints on authentication system design. 


          To address these challenges, I propose a novel authentication system specifically designed for mobile
          devices. Our system leverages the advantages of multi-factor authentication and biometric techniques 
          to provide a robust and secure authentication mechanism. In this paper, I discuss the background and
          motivations for our research, review the existing literature on authentication systems, present the
          methodology and implementation details of our system, evaluate its performance, and conclude with 
          insights and future research directions.
